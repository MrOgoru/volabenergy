About Us

Volab Energy and Systems Limited is a subsidiary of Rehoboth Systems Limited which is wholly indigenous company that majors in the supply of IT Power Infrastructure, & Power Management solutions to diverse sectors of the economy.

We have developed competencies and strategic relationships with Original Equipment Manufacturers such as Eaton, Makelsan, Schneider and other OEM. As an organization that prioritizes the interests of its stakeholders, we pride ourselves in the quality of services we rovide our clients.
